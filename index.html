Follower Ledger

Compares Instagram's own data export against a snapshot you saved last time, so you can see who unfollowed you and who doesn't follow you back — without logging in anywhere or automating your account. Nothing here is uploaded anywhere; every file stays on your machine.
Getting your data from Instagram (do this each time you want to check):
  1. Open Instagram → Settings → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions → Download your information
  2. Choose Some of your information → select ONLY Followers and following
  3. Set format to JSON, media quality to be Lower quality, submit, and wait for Instagram's email/notification with the download
  4. Unzip the file — you'll find followers_1.json and following.json inside the connections folder
